**DBaaS**

Pros:

- Easy to setup, fully managed.
- Built-in backups.
- Integrated with Google Cloud Platform and their services.
- High availability and reliability.

Cons:

- Higher costs per resource.
- Less flexibility and customization.

**DIY**

Pros:

- Full control.
- Potentially lower costs.

Cons:

- Complex setup and maintenance efforts.
- Must handle backups ourselves.
- Higher risk of downtime.
